Work With Me

A simple, transparent process from consultation to final delivery

01

Free Consultation

We'll discuss your manuscript, your goals, your timeline, and which editing service best fits your needs. This is a no-pressure conversation to see if we're a good fit. No commitment required!

02

Free Sample Edit

I'll edit a sample of your manuscript (typically 1-2 chapters or 2,000-5,000 words) for free. This lets you see my editing style in action and decide if it's the right match for your voice and vision.

03

Proposal & Agreement

You'll receive a detailed quote with timeline, deliverables, and payment terms. I offer flexible payment plans up to 12 months to make professional editing accessible. Once you're ready, we'll finalize the agreement and schedule your project.

04

Editing Process

I work through your manuscript with care and attention to detail. You'll receive regular updates on progress, and I'm always available if questions arise. Communication is key to a successful partnership!

05

Delivery & Revisions

Receive your edited manuscript with tracked changes, margin comments, and (for developmental edits) a comprehensive editorial letter. One round of revisions is included to ensure you're completely satisfied with the work.
